Blood Donation management System (BBMS) is a browser based system that is designed to store, process, retrieve and analyze information concerned with the administrative and inventorymanagement within a blood bank. This project aims at maintaining all the information pertainingto blood donors, different blood groups available in each blood bank and help them manage in a better way. Aim is to provide transparency in this field, make the process of obtaining bloodfrom a blood bank hassle free and corruption free and make the system of blood bankmanagement effective.
